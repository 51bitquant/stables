Our Services

We walk alongside people at their point of need, offering practical support and spiritual guidance.

About The Stables

Founded in 1993 by Sonny and Lynne Hoet, The Stables began with a vision inspired by Luke 4:18 to preach good news to the poor, heal the broken hearted, and bring freedom and restoration to those in need.

That vision is still at the heart of everything we do today. Located on 33 acres at the edge of suburbia in the City of Onkaparinga, we've created a welcoming space where individuals and families can find help, hope, and healing.

Whether you're looking for spiritual growth, emotional support, or practical help, The Stables is a place where you'll be seen, supported, and encouraged.
